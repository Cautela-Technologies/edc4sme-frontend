Program Overview

Over 6 weeks, dive into hands-on workshops covering market analysis, business modeling, cash-flow management, and legal essentials. By program’s end, you’ll have a fully vetted plan and the confidence to launch or scale.

The Growth Enterprise Programme is a blended (Online and in-class) program tailored for early-stage businesses and startups. It's designed to guide them in identifying steps to expand their businesses and make them more attractive for funding. Navigating the dynamic landscape of growing a startup requires expertise in strategy, business models, marketing, and team building to realise your vision.

Program Objectives

  • To engage those who are considering the venturing process to have a smooth learning experience while still in employment.
  • To set the foundation for the implementation of suitable systems and structures to ensure long-term sustainability of entrepreneurial ventures.
  • To help the Entrepreneur and her organisation to be better prepared in accessing finance for growth.
  • Provide the networks for access to new markets to deliver valuable products and services.

Eligibility Criteria

  • Business must be a start up
  • You should be an early Stage Businesses
