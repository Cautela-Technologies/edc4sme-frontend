About EDC4SME

At EDC4SME, we’re on a mission to democratise business education across Africa. Our digital platform brings together curated learning journeys, live coaching, and real-time application tracking— so you can focus on what matters: building your dream.

Mission

Make high-quality SME support accessible, affordable, and actionable.

Vision

A vibrant network of empowered entrepreneurs driving inclusive economic growth.

Objectives

Streamline discovery, application, and enrolment. Deliver hands-on learning with real business impact. Equip facilitators and admins with intuitive tools.
