Privacy Policy

Effective Date: 5th August, 2025

1.0 Who we are

At Enterprise Development Centre (EDC), we value the trust our users and corporate clients place in us. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, disclose, and protect your personal information when you engage with our online services.

Our Address:

Enterprise Development Centre

Km 22, Lekki-Epe Expressway,

Lekki, Lagos, Nigeria.

2.0 What Personal Information Do We Collect?

We may collect the following types of personally identifiable information when you use our services, contact us, or apply for a program:

  • Full name, nationality, gender, date of birth, and religion
  • Contact details (address, email, phone number)
  • Communication preferences
  • Employment details (organization, job title, experience, industry)
  • Educational background (institutions, course, year of graduation)
  • Business Registration Number
  • National Identification Number (NIN)
  • Usage Data, including: IP address, Browser type and version, Pages visited and time spent, Access times and dates, Device identifiers and diagnostic data.

3.0 How Do We Use Your Information?

Your information may be used for the following purposes:

  • To communicate updates about our programs and services
  • To offer customer support and respond to inquiries
  • To personalise and improve user experience
  • To analyse the effectiveness of our outreach efforts
  • To follow up after prior communication
  • To maintain technical performance and security

We will never use your personal data for unrelated purposes without prior notice and consent.

4.0 Will EDC Share Your Personal Information?

We do not sell or share your personal information with third parties, except:

  • When required by law
  • During partnership programmes (e.g., sponsored programs like Providus Bank SME Program), where a clear notice will be provided

Your information remains confidential and secure under all other circumstances.

5.0 Cookies and How We Use Them

Cookies are small files stored on your device containing anonymous identifiers. We use cookies to:

  • Remember your preferences
  • Improve your browsing experience based on past and current activity
  • Enhance the performance of our website

You can choose to disable cookies through your browser settings.

6.0 Your Rights Over Your Personal Information

You have the right to:

  • Request access to your data (without violating another's privacy)
  • Correct or update inaccuracies
  • Request deletion of your data (we may retain minimal info to avoid future contact)
  • Withdraw consent at any time
  • Request a copy of your data in a transferable format
  • Restrict how your data is processed

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at edc@pau.edu.ng

7.0 Updating Your Information

Please notify us of any changes to your personal information to ensure our records remain accurate. You may contact edc@pau.edu.ng

8.0 Policy Updates

We may revise this Privacy Policy periodically. Users will be notified via email and/or a prominent notice on our website prior to changes taking effect. The updated policy will be posted here with a new effective date .
